Electrical Engineering Intern

REM Electromach Pvt. Ltd.

During my tenure as an Electrical Engineering intern at REM Electromech Pvt Ltd, I gained valuable work experience and honed my skills in electrical design, with a particular focus on automation projects involving Automation Panels, Electronic Controls, PLC (Programmable Logic Controller), HMI (Human Machine Interface), and Mimic diagrams. Working closely with the engineering team, I actively contributed to the development and implementation of automation solutions, leveraging my technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities. This hands-on experience allowed me to enhance my understanding of electrical systems, their design principles, and the integration of automation technologies.

Graduate Electrical Engineer

Torrent Power

As a Graduate Electrical Engineer at Torrent Power, I have excelled in effective team communication, ensuring up-to-date information reaches all members. I actively participate in developing team goals and maintain regular communication with technical and non-technical teams, monitoring production progress. My expertise lies in designing, developing, and testing electronic equipment and systems, focusing on communication, electro-optics, signal processing, and control circuitry to enhance reliability. With strong skills in electrical wiring, troubleshooting, and power systems, I have contributed to safety and performance engineering calculations. My experience at Torrent Power has been marked by growth, collaboration, and the application of my electrical engineering skills.
