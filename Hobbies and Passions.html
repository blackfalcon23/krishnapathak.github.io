Hobbies and Passions

Dance has always been a passion of mine, particularly the classical Indian dance form of Bharatnatyam. I have been drawn to Bharatnatyam ever since I was a child, captivated by the intricate footwork, graceful hand gestures, and expressive storytelling. It was a form of escape and expression for me, allowing me to immerse myself in the music and movements, and convey emotions that words alone could not.

My passion for Bharatnatyam only grew stronger over the years, leading me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in the dance form from Gandharv Mahavidyalay. During my studies, I delved deep into the history and theory of Bharatnatyam, learning about its origins, traditions, and various interpretations. I also received rigorous training in the technique and performance aspects of the dance form, honing my skills and developing a greater understanding of the art.

Today, Bharatnatyam continues to be an integral part of my life, as I seek to share my love and knowledge of the dance form with others. I have performed in numerous cultural events and competitions, and have also had the privilege of teaching Bharatnatyam to students of all ages and backgrounds. For me, Bharatnatyam is not just a hobby or a skill, but a way of life, a means of connecting with my heritage and expressing myself through the beauty of movement and music.
